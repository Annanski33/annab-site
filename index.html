About Me

Hi, my name is Anna. I am a front-end web developer. I specialize in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, JQuery, PHP, WordPress, and I am constantly learning new skills. I live in Toronto, Canada with my fiancé Chris and our cat Max.

Before I learned to program I worked as a designer, video editor, accounting/administrative assistant, and even retail manager. It was all great fun! I was always tech-savvy though, and I knew that I would become a developer.
