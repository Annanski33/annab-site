About Me

Hello, my name is Anna. Since 2019 I've been working as a freelance designer and front-end developer for different clients. I specialize in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP, Wordpress, and I am constantly learning new skills. I live in Toronto, Canada with my fiance Chris and our cat Max.

Before my freelancing, I was working as a purchaser, administrative assistant, accountant, and retail clerk.

I love spending my free time in nature with a small group of friends, read, take pictures, and eat good food.

Skills

My focus is to write clean, well-formatted code to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot, and build on. I have experience editing and updating existing sites and dynamic web applications, and I do my best to stay up to date on current best practices.
